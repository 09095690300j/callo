Make Your Dream in Reality: Become a HUMSS Student

Are you a student who is passionate about the humanities, social sciences, and literature? Do you enjoy exploring the depths of human behavior, understanding the intricacies of society, and delving into the world of language and culture? If so, then a HUMSS (HUManities and Social Sciences) strand might be the perfect fit for you.

HUMSS is a diverse and dynamic field that encompasses a wide range of subjects, including history, psychology, sociology, anthropology, philosophy, and foreign languages. HUMSS students are equipped with the critical thinking skills, analytical abilities, and communication skills necessary to succeed in a variety of careers. They are also prepared to engage with global issues, promote social justice, and contribute to the betterment of society.

Why Choose HUMSS?

  • Develop critical thinking skills
  • Gain a deeper understanding of society
  • Enhance communication skills
  • Explore diverse cultures
  • Prepare for a variety of careers

Career Prospects

HUMSS graduates are well-positioned for a wide range of careers in both the public and private sectors. Some of the potential career paths include:

  • Education
  • Social work
  • Journalism
  • Law
  • Government
  • Business
  • International relations
  • Non-profit organizations
